Description

Brigadeiro is a traditional Brazilian delicacy similar to a chocolate truffle. Made from sweetened condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter, it's a staple at every Brazilian celebration.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk (395g)
  • 4 tablespoons of 50% cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. In a deep saucepan, add all the ingredients and mix well.
  2. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until the mixture pulls away from the bottom of the pan (about 10 minutes).
  3. Remove from the heat, transfer to a buttered plate, and let it cool.
