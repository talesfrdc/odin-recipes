Pudim

Description

Pudim is a Brazilian caramel flan, smooth and creamy with a rich caramel topping.

Ingredients

Pudim

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of milk (use the condensed milk can to measure)
  • 3 whole eggs

Caramel Sauce

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

  1. First, blend the eggs well in a blender.
  2. Add the sweetened condensed milk and the milk, then blend again.
  3. Melt the sugar in a pan until golden brown, add the water, and let it thicken.
  4. Pour the caramel into a round baking pan, then pour the pudding mixture on top.
  5. Bake in a medium oven for about 45 minutes, placing the round pan inside a larger one with water (water bath).
  6. Insert a fork to check if it is fully baked.
  7. Let it cool and remove from the pan.

Source: Tudo Gostoso
